Project 2: Nmap Recon on VulnHub VM

Summary

Performed reconnaissance and enumeration on a local VulnHub VM using Nmap and NSE scripts. Identified open ports and mapped services to potential vulnerabilities.

Outcomes

  • Automated scans with a custom NSE profile and parsed results to Markdown.
  • Mapped services to potential CVEs and documented attack surface.

Tools Used

Nmap, NSE scripts, local VulnHub VM.
